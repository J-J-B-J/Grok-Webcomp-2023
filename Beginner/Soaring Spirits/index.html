Karpathos Island, Greece

Can you imagine a more beautiful place?

I am basically in love with Karpathos — the people here are amazing and so hospitable.

And every corner you turn there is a view like this!

There are twelve villages here, some perched up on the mountain, and some sprawled by the sea. I think I love the mountain places the best so far — they cling joyfully to the sides of the mountain like an explosion of colourful boxes!

"Colour makes my heart soar ..."

I think I’ve seen every colour of the rainbow here — even the ocean is glowing with that classic jade green and deep clear blues of the Mediterranean. Maybe when I get back home I’ll paint from front door a vibrant red or orange, or deep blue, to remind me of this place …

… but not today! I can’t wait to stop here at this cafe for a long, cool drink. Hiking up here has given me an epic thirst!
