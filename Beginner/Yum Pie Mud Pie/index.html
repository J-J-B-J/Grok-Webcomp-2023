Mud Pie Recipe

4.9 stars • 283 reviews • 20 min • 4 servings • Easy

Ingredients

• Dirt

• Water

• Leaves, sticks, etc. to taste

• Small pebbles

• Flowers for decoration

Method

Mix dirt and water in a bucket until it's the consistency of playdough, adding leaves and sticks to taste.

Roll into balls and flatten into pies.

Add a crust by lining the edges with small pebbles.

Top with flowers. Serve cold.
